From clicking through a tiny encyclopedia to navigating the largest knowledge network ever built, how does the pathing changes? 

This project aims to study and characterize the impact of two important variables over the Wikispeedia players behavior : 
- the positions of the links in the articles
- the size of the Wikipedia version they are playing on. 

We believe, and will verify, that the positional [structure of Wikipedia]({{ '/story/1_a' | relative_url }}) did not move much over the year : links are still in the same locations, we have same ratio of hubs, of isolated articlesâ€¦ and the size of wikipedia instead grew a lot since the 2007 Wikispeedia dataset. 

By analyzing these two aspects, we want to understand how a player in 2025 would behave if Wikispeedia was played on the full modern Wikipedia. Our goal is to estimate how navigation strategies would adapt and what level of performance could be expected today. Come with us on this journey !

To better understand what it is like to play wikispeedia on a smaller graph, you have to find your way through our story by navigating the hyperlinks, just like a wikispeedia game!
